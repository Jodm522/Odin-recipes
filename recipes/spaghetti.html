Spaghetti

Spaghetti is pretty good, here's a pretty good recipe for it.
  • 1 Small can of tomato paste
  • 32 oz San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 medium yellow onion, peeled and diced
  • 3 Garlic cloves, peeled and diced
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 sprig of basil
  1. Place the olive oul in a pan over medium heat, once at heat add the onions and cook until slightly translucent
  2. add the garlic and red pepper flake, saute until fragrant
  3. add the tomato paste and mix to combine, continue sauteeing the mixture, giving the paste 5-6 minutes to begin to caramelize
  4. Add the tomatoes, stir, and crush until the sauce is smooth
  5. add the basil, salt , and pepper, and allow the sauce to simmer for at least an hour, up to 6
