Steak

It's a steak. You're welcome
  • Ribeye steak
  • 2-3 cloves of garic
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  1. Salt and pepper the steak to taste, allow it to rest for 30mins
  2. Heat a pan over meduim-high heat
  3. Place the steak in the pan, flipping every 2-3 mins
  4. Cook to desired doneness, 2-3 mins before completion place the butter and garlic in the pan, spooning the butter over the steak until complete
  5. pull the steak from the pan, allowing it to rest for at least 5 mins before eating
