Martini

Here's a drink recipe I guess, just don't go nuts with it
  • Gin, amount and style to your taste
  • Green Olives
  • Chilled Martini glass (or whatever you have laying round)
  • Ice
  • Unopened bottle of vermouth
  1. Stir the gin and ice together until chilled while giving passing glace at the bottle of vermouth
  2. Strain the gin into your chilled drink receptacle
  3. Toss in the olive if you're into that
